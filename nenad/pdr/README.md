Project-level decisions: scope, product, process, and priorities. Distinct from **ADRs** (technical/architecture) in `nenad/adr/`.

## Naming

- `NNN-short-slug.md` (e.g. `001-scope-february-automation.md`).

## Suggested template

```markdown
# PDR NNN: Short title

## Status
Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated

## Context
Why this decision is needed (goals, constraints).

## Decision
What we decided (scope, product, process).

## Consequences
Impact, follow-ups, what to update.
```
