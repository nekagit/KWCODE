## Design Plan: Simplify Idea-Driven Night Shift Workflow

### Current Behavior

The "Idea-driven" button opens a dialog with **two options**:
1. **"Use existing ideas and tickets"** — fetches existing ideas from DB and runs circle for each ticket
2. **"Create from description"** — textarea + "Create & run" button that creates 1 new idea → 1 milestone → 1 ticket → runs circle

### Requested Change

Simplify to **only** the "Create from description" workflow:
- Remove the "Use existing ideas and tickets" button
- Keep the textarea where user writes the idea
- When done, creates exactly 1 new idea and runs the circle
